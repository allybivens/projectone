THE THREE AREAS
The park includes an area that is restricted for big dogs, a similiar area for smaller dogs, and an off-leash trail area. Checkout the locations section of our page for a map of these areas.

LEASHED AREAS
Keep your dog’s leash with you at all times and always have a collar on your dog. When entering and exiting the park, dogs must remain on the leash. It is at the owner’s discretion if they allow their dogs off the leash in any other part of the park. The NorthLake Dog Park will not be held liable for any dog’s actions.
LARGE & SMALL
There are separated areas for large and small dogs to ensure the safety of your dog while in the park. The separation helps ensure that both the dogs and their owners are able to enjoy their time at the park and also provides an opportunity to meet the needs of all park users, regardless of the size of their pets. In both areas, there is access to water fountains and shady areas. Please read the signs in the park in order to guide your decision in where your dog will play.
OFF-LEASH TRAILS
This park is unique because it features trails where dogs are not required to be leashed. This area is gated to take the stress away and ensure no one gets lost. If your dog is dog agressive or disobedient, please refrain from going leashless. However, it is at the owner’s discretion if they allow their dogs off the leash.
