WHY NORTHLAKE DOG PARK IS SPECIAL

The Northlake Dog Park is a 12-acre park located on North White Street in Rock Hill. It has four major areas that are further described and show in the features section of our page. It was opened in August of 2014, becoming the first off-leash dog park in Rock Hill.

Users of the dog park are encouraged to read the dog park rules before usage of the park.




NORTHLAKE DOG PARK RULES:

- Must have a Northlake Dog Park Pass for access to this park. (Memberships are $15.99 a month)
- All dogs must be on leashes when entering and exiting the park.
- All dogs must have ID tags on.
- All dogs must be spayed/neutered.
- Park hours are 6:30am - 10 pm daily.
-Owners must dispose of waste properly in the designated on-site containers.
-Owners are liable for any injury or damage caused by their dog and are fully responsible for their dogs actions.
